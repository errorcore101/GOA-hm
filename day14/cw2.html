madness combat

Madness Combat is a long-running animated web series created by Matt Jolly (Krinkels) on Newgrounds. The series features the protagonist Hank J. Wimbleton in violent, adrenaline-fueled battles with various characters in the surreal, dystopian setting of Nevada, often with a focus on mass killing and intense action. The series is known for its distinctive visual style, simple character designs, and a surprisingly intricate lore that has evolved over time, spawning many fan tributes and even games.
