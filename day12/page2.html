OTHER DETAILS

Origin: The series started on the Newgrounds website in 2002, a platform where creators shared their content for enjoyment. Creator: Created by Matt Jolly, also known as Krinkels. Setting: Takes place in a surreal and violent world, initially believed to be Nevada, where characters often have cross-like faces. Characters: Features a core cast, with Hank J. Wimbleton as the main anti-hero, who engages in relentless battles against various antagonists. Action & Style: The series is characterized by its high-octane, gore-filled action sequences and a distinct, minimalist art style that became iconic. Community: Has a large and active fan base, celebrated on Newgrounds through Madness Day on September 22, where fans submit their own works. Evolution: While initially dismissed by some as having no plot, the series has developed a complex lore, with official games like MADNESS: Project Nexus further exploring its intricate world.
