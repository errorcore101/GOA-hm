puss in boots

Puss in Boots is a 2011 animated film that serves as a prequel to the Shrek series, focusing on the titular character's origin story before he meets Shrek and Donkey. Antonio Banderas reprises his role as the swashbuckling feline, who, alongside his childhood friend Humpty Dumpty (Zach Galifianakis) and the sly Kitty Softpaws (Salma Hayek), embarks on a quest to steal magic beans from Jack and Jill to get the fabled Golden Goose and clear his name. The film was a critical and commercial success, praised for its action-adventure and comedy, and introduced Puss's first romantic interest, Kitty Softpaws.

kung fu panda(peak)

Kung Fu Panda is a 2008 animated adventure film from DreamWorks Animation about Po, a clumsy panda who, despite working in his father's noodle shop, dreams of being a kung fu master. He is unexpectedly chosen to become the Dragon Warrior, the most skilled fighter in the land, and must train with Master Shifu and the Furious Five to defeat the villainous Tai Lung and save the Valley of Peace. The film is known for its blend of humor and action, with stunning animation inspired by Chinese art and films.

legend of aang(2026)

The animated film, titled The Legend of Aang: The Last Airbender, is an upcoming theatrical release from Avatar Studios, scheduled for October 9, 2026, by Paramount Pictures. The film will focus on Avatar Aang and his friends in their adult years, featuring the director of The Legend of Korra, Lauren Montgomery, and starring actors like Eric Nam as Aang.
